Welcome to the Multnomah County Animal Shelter

The Multnomah County Animal rescuses, heals and adopts more than 11,000 pets each year. We never place a limit on how long cats, dogs, and other pets stay at our shelter, MCAS relies on donations to support our adoption, education, and animal cruelty investigation programs.
